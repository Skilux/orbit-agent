Track 6 — Cross-MCP Orchestration Team Rohlik Hermes · FastMCP · Bloomreach
The challenge

Lean teams, rich data,
not enough hours.

📊
Discovery is manual
Finding a real revenue opportunity in Bloomreach takes hours of EQL, funnel analysis, consent checks, and scenario auditing — before any campaign brief is even drafted.
🔁
The cycle is fragmented
Discover → build → send → measure → iterate. Each step lives in a different tool, owned by a different person. The loop breaks every handoff. Insights expire.
Orbit's thesis

thin tools + rich agent knowledge = leverage.

Orbit reads Bloomreach with Loomi Connect, operates existing infra through a deliberately thin write-MCP, and creates whole campaigns from its own skills — the agent is the intelligence, not the tools.

System architecture

Two MCP surfaces. Two trust levels. One human gate each.

Responsible AI note
